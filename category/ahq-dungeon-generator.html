AHQ Dungeon Generator

Published: Mon 01 August 2022
By LaochraQuest

In AHQ Dungeon Generator.

The Advanced HeroQuest Dungeon Generator provides a quick and clean method for generating your dungeon.

This allows quick generation of passages and rooms. It also provides a roll for the Treasure table and where a Room door leads (i.e. to another room or passage).

By default Quest rooms are discovered with the Terror in the Dark rules, i.e. you cannot find a Quest room until you've discovered at least 3 rooms and the chance increases with the number of rooms you discover. This can be turned off or the room counter can be reset at any point.

In future I would like to add the Monster Tables from Terror in the Dark as well as the Adventure Tables.
